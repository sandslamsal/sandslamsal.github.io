From 2017 to 2022, worked as a Structural Engineer at Tensile Structure Pvt. Ltd., Kathmandu, Nepal, where I led procurement and contract management for major infrastructure projects, securing over $600,000 in contracts within the first two years. Designed and supervised a wide range of projects, including high-rise RC buildings, commercial steel structures, prestressed and RCC bridges, steel arch trusses, and long-span pedestrian suspension bridges, ensuring compliance with IS and NBC standards. Oversaw all phases from survey and design to construction supervision, delivering projects within budget, on schedule, and to quality standards.
